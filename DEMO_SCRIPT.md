# MindCraft AI — 5-Minute Demo Script

**Target Audience:** IBM Judges, Recruiters, Technical Evaluators.  
**Objective:** Prove that MindCraft AI solves passive learning by forcing active recall and adaptive revision.

---

## Preparation (Before you screen-share)
1. Have the application running on `localhost:5500` (or production URL).
2. Have a sample PDF ready (e.g., a 2-page biology text about Cell Division).
3. Ensure the browser is full-screen.

---

## 0:00 – The Hook & Introduction
*(Screen shows the empty Home tab)*
**Speaker:** "Hello! How many times have you read a 10-page textbook chapter, only to realize at the end that you don't remember a single thing you just read? We call this 'passive consumption'. Today, I'm going to show you MindCraft AI—an intelligent workspace that completely eliminates passive reading by forcing you into an active learning loop."

## 0:45 – PDF Extraction & Notes
*(Action: Click 'Choose File' and upload the sample PDF. Wait for the text to appear.)*
**Speaker:** "It starts with your material. I'm uploading a dense PDF on cellular biology. Behind the scenes, we extract the raw text."

*(Action: Click 'Generate Notes'. Wait for the result.)*
**Speaker:** "Instead of asking you to read a wall of text, our Gemini-powered engine instantly restructures it. Notice how it breaks the material down into Digestible Headings, Key Points, Definitions, and even Memory Tricks. This isn't just summarizing; this is educational scaffolding."

## 1:45 – Active Recall (Flashcards)
*(Action: Click the 'Flashcards' tab. Set count to 5. Click 'Generate Flashcards'.)*
**Speaker:** "But reading notes is still passive. True retention comes from Active Recall. With one click, MindCraft AI generates an interactive flashcard deck based *exactly* on the text we just uploaded."

*(Action: Click a flashcard to flip it. Click 'Next Card'.)*
**Speaker:** "I can test myself instantly. The UI is built with zero-latency Vanilla JavaScript, meaning there's no lag between my thoughts and the interface."

## 2:45 – Dynamic Testing & The "Aha!" Moment
*(Action: Click the 'Quiz' tab. Generate a 3-question quiz.)*
**Speaker:** "Flashcards are great, but let's prove what we actually know. MindCraft AI builds a multiple-choice quiz."

*(Action: Deliberately answer 1 question CORRECTLY, and 2 questions INCORRECTLY. Click 'Submit Quiz'.)*
**Speaker:** "I'm going to intentionally get a couple of these wrong to show you the most powerful feature of this platform."

## 3:45 – Adaptive Revision (The Killer Feature)
*(Action: Scroll down to the Quiz Results section showing 'Weak Concepts'.)*
**Speaker:** "Notice what happened. MindCraft AI didn't just give me a score. It analyzed my incorrect answers and extracted my **Weak Concepts**. It knows I don't understand 'Mitosis phases'."

*(Action: Click 'Test Weak Topics'.)*
**Speaker:** "Now, instead of re-reading the entire chapter, I click 'Test Weak Topics'. The system builds a brand new, highly targeted retest *specifically* focused on the areas I failed. This is adaptive, personalized learning in real-time."

## 4:30 – Conclusion & Polish
*(Action: Click the 'Notes' tab. Toggle the 'Dark Theme' button. Click 'Export PDF'.)*
**Speaker:** "Finally, I can switch to Dark Mode for late-night studying, and if I need to go offline, I can export my generated notes to a beautifully formatted PDF. MindCraft AI: turning passive reading into active mastery. Thank you."
