# MindCraft AI — Screenshot Capture Guide

To make the GitHub `README.md` and your portfolio look world-class, you must capture high-quality screenshots. Follow these exact instructions.

## General Rules for All Screenshots
1. **Resolution:** Use a desktop monitor at 1920x1080 resolution.
2. **Browser:** Use Chrome or Edge. Hide the bookmarks bar (`Ctrl+Shift+B`).
3. **Zoom:** Keep browser zoom exactly at 100%.
4. **Framing:** Do NOT include your OS taskbar. Use a tool like Mac's `Cmd+Shift+4` or Windows Snipping Tool to capture *only* the browser window content (or use a browser extension like GoFullPage).
5. **Data:** Upload a real, academic PDF (e.g., a Wikipedia PDF on "Quantum Mechanics" or "Photosynthesis") so the generated content looks highly intelligent. Do not use "test test test".

## Screenshot 1: The Workspace Dashboard (Hero Shot)
**Purpose:** Show the clean UI and the generated notes.
- **Theme:** Academic (Light Mode).
- **Setup:** 
  1. Upload your academic PDF.
  2. Click "Generate Notes" and wait for it to finish.
  3. Ensure the left sidebar (source text) and right panel (beautifully rendered notes with headings, bold text, and bullet points) are both visible.
- **Framing:** Capture the entire browser viewport (edge to edge).

## Screenshot 2: The Dark Theme & Flashcards
**Purpose:** Highlight the dark mode aesthetic and the active recall feature.
- **Theme:** Dark Mode (Toggle it on).
- **Setup:**
  1. Go to the **Flashcards** tab.
  2. Generate 5 flashcards.
  3. Click the first flashcard so it flips to the **BACK** (showing the answer).
  4. Hover your mouse over the "Next Card" button so its hover-state color activates.
- **Framing:** Crop slightly tighter around the central flashcard widget and the tab navigation bar.

## Screenshot 3: The Dynamic Quiz & Weak Concepts
**Purpose:** Demonstrate the adaptive learning engine.
- **Theme:** Academic (Light Mode).
- **Setup:**
  1. Go to the **Quiz** tab and generate a quiz.
  2. Answer the quiz. **Intentionally get at least 2 questions wrong.**
  3. Click "Submit Quiz".
  4. Scroll down so the "Quiz Results" box is dead center in the screen.
  5. Ensure the red "Incorrect" markers and the "Weak Concepts Identified" tags are clearly visible.
- **Framing:** Focus on the results box and the "Test Weak Topics" button.

## Screenshot 4: PDF Export Quality
**Purpose:** Prove that the application generates high-quality printable assets.
- **Setup:**
  1. Go to the **Notes** tab.
  2. Click "Export PDF".
  3. Open the downloaded PDF in your system's default PDF viewer (e.g., Adobe Acrobat or Mac Preview).
- **Framing:** Take a screenshot of the PDF viewer itself, showing the crisp typography and layout of the generated notes on a standard A4 page.
